CMS FY 2026 Hospital Readmissions & Patient Experience Dashboard

Executive-ready healthcare quality dashboard using current CMS Provider Data. It connects readmission penalty exposure, patient experience, hospital ratings, and unplanned visit signals into one management view.

Management Story

This dashboard answers one practical question: which hospitals show elevated readmission pressure, weaker patient experience, or broader quality concerns? It is designed for leaders who need a fast view before asking analysts for deeper drill-down.
  • Readmission pressure: hospitals with excess readmission ratios above 1.0 perform worse than expected after risk adjustment.
  • Patient trust: HCAHPS recommendation scores show whether patients would definitely recommend the hospital.
  • Prioritization: a simple portfolio priority score combines readmission, rating, patient experience, and unplanned visit signals.

How to Read This

Upper-left hospitals are the most concerning: readmission ratios are high while patient recommendation is low. Lower-right hospitals are generally stronger: lower readmission pressure and better patient trust.

This view is intentionally executive-friendly: it does not start with raw clinical detail. It starts with the operational question leaders care about: where should we investigate first?
